Early Beginnings

Traditional animation, also known as hand-drawn animation, involves drawing each frame by hand. This process was used in early classics like Steamboat Willie (1928) and Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs (1937).

Techniques Used

  • Cel animation using transparent sheets
  • Frame-by-frame drawing
  • Rotoscoping for realistic motion
